# PolyAlerts — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 8 July 2026_

PolyAlerts ("the app") is an unofficial, open-source companion app that shows
public Polymarket prediction-market prices and lets you set local price alerts.
This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

## Summary

**PolyAlerts does not collect, store, or share any personal data.** There is no
account, no login, and no analytics or tracking. Everything the app stores stays
on your device.

## Information the app handles

- **Your alerts and settings.** The markets you follow and the price alerts you
  create are stored **only on your device**, in the app's local storage. They
  are never uploaded to us or to any third party.
- **No personal identifiers.** The app does not ask for or collect your name,
  email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal
  information.
- **No analytics or advertising.** The app contains no analytics SDKs, no
  advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

## Network connections

The app makes network requests only to:

- **Polymarket's public API** (`gamma-api.polymarket.com`) to fetch public
  market prices and images. These requests contain no personal information about
  you — only the standard data any app sends when requesting a web resource.
- **Polymarket's website**, when you choose to open a market. Tapping through to
  a market opens it in your browser or an in-app browser tab; from that point
  Polymarket's own privacy policy applies.

The app has no backend server of its own and sends your data to no one.

## Sharing alerts (QR codes)

If you use the "Send"/"Receive" feature to copy alerts between two phones, the
alert data is encoded into a **QR code shown on your own screen** and read by the
other phone's camera. This transfer happens **directly between the two devices**
(one shows a code, the other scans it). No data is sent over the internet or to
any server during this process.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — used only to scan a PolyAlerts QR code when you tap "Receive". The
  camera is not used at any other time, and no images are stored or transmitted.
- **Notifications** — used to alert you when a market reaches a price you set.
- **Internet** — used to fetch public market prices from Polymarket's API.

## Children

The app is not directed at children. It displays financial/prediction-market
information intended for adults.

## Data deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you can delete everything the app holds by
clearing the app's storage or uninstalling it. There is no server-side data to
request deletion of.

## Not affiliated with Polymarket

PolyAlerts is an independent, unofficial project and is not affiliated with,
endorsed by, or connected to Polymarket. "Polymarket" is a trademark of its
respective owner.
